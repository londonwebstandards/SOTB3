What is State of the Browser?

Representatives from four major browser vendors will be on hand to walk attendees through their upcoming new releases. You'll get the skinny on how they've implemented exciting new technologies and find out where the web is going at breakneck speed. Through keynotes, Q&As, breakout sessions, workshops and socialising, you'll get a better understanding of the browser in 2011 than ever before.

Who Is This Event For?

State Of The Browser is being organised by London Webstandards, the group for London's creative web industry. We welcome anyone with an interest in creating content for the next generation of browsers. Whether you're an HTML5 ninja, an IA looking to understand what new experiences are being opened up or you dream in HTTP, this event will give you the inside track on what promises to be one of the most exciting years yet for the browser.
